CYBER SELF-DEFENSE
by : Rodrigo Bonzerr S. Lopez 2020

The fast evolving technology industry entails
with it the threat of being exposed to a lot
of vulnerabilities and exploits.

Almost everyone now a days are involved into using gadgets and
technologies that can make them a susceptible victim to cyber criminals.
Learning how to protect yourself against the different attacks that
these cyber criminals can greatly help ensure the safety of your personal data.
These applies for businesses that needs to ensure the security of their data and their clients data
and ordinary individuals who are involved into using techonological devices.
The goal is to protect themselves against atrocious elements out in the cyber world.
